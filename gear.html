Fishing Gear!
Gear Brief Description / Uses Photo (hover over any image to enlarge)
Tackle Box A Tackle Box is a box or container used for the storage of fishing supplies.
Many fisherman carry things line extra line, bobbers, weights, hooks, etc.
Fishing Pole A Fishing Pole is used commonly to catch fish.
A fishing pole could be any kind of pole with string and a hook on it that you could use to catch a fish.
Fishing Line Fishing Line is attached to a fishing pole,
When a fish bites on the hook attached to the other end of the line,
it is the fishing line's job to not break under pressure of the fish.
There are diffrent line thicknesses and strengths for diffrent kinds of fish.
Hook Hooks are attached to the fishing line,
This is the piece of equipment that will hook the fish and make it catchable.
Hook sizes vary depending on the size of fish you are fishing for.
Bobber Bobbers are devices that you attach to fishing line.
When you cast into the water, the bobber will float, thus suspending your hook underwater but not allowing it to sink to the bottem.
There are a couple diffrent kinds of bobbers, but the concept very simular for them all.
Reel Reels are devices that are attached to both the fishing pole and fishing line
 There is a handle on the reel, and when you spin it it will neatly reel in your fishing line, or cast more out.
This makes it possible to set the length of line you want to let out, instead of always having a certain amount of line out.
Weights Many fishermen attach Weights to their fishing line near the hook and below the bobber.
This assures that the fishing line will sink to the lowest point it can go, it also allows you to cast farther.
